What Plottie does

Two audiences.
One product.

Teams keep the roadmap alive. Leadership gets answers without opening another tool. Both get what they need without a status meeting.

📅
Multi-year Gantt
Tasks span months or years with weekly granularity. Continuation pips mark cross-year extents. Today indicator. Overdue highlighting.
🤖
Conversational AI
Ask your roadmap anything in plain English. "What's at risk this quarter?" gets an instant answer — not a Jira query. Powered by Mistral on EU infrastructure.
📬
Monday digest
Automated plain English summary emailed every Monday morning. Leadership stays informed without logging in. No chasing, no status decks.
🔒
Project visibility controls
Mark projects as private, team-wide, or restricted. Admins see everything. Members see what they need to. Simple and enforceable.
🗒️
Notes & blockers
Attach notes, risks, and blockers to any task. Filter by type, owner, or task. Every change carries an audit trail — who did what, when.

Teams & Slack bot
Ask roadmap questions directly from Teams or Slack. Leadership gets answers in the tools they already live in — no new tab required.

Questions

Frequently asked.

What counts as an AI query? ↓
Any question you ask your roadmap in plain English — "what's at risk this quarter?", "what is Alice working on?", "summarise last month". Each question is one query. The 10 monthly queries on Team tier reset on your billing date. Clarity and Control have unlimited queries.
Does the AI see my roadmap data? ↓
Yes, but only your tenant's data. When you ask a question, your server fetches your data and sends it to Mistral's EU infrastructure for processing. Mistral does not retain or train on API query content. On the Control plan, the AI model runs on your own dedicated server — nothing leaves your VPS.
How is this different from Asana or Monday? ↓
Asana manages task execution — who does what, by when. Plottie communicates planning intent — what are we building, roughly when, who owns it. Most teams use both: Asana for daily task management, Plottie for quarterly planning visibility and leadership communication. We also run entirely on EU infrastructure, which rules out Asana for many regulated-industry buyers.
Can I migrate from an existing tool? ↓
Yes. Plottie supports CSV import for tasks and projects. If you're on an older single-tenant Plottie instance, we provide a migration script that moves your projects, tasks, and notes into the new platform with historical audit trail preserved.
Do you support invoice billing for procurement? ↓
Yes, on the Control plan. We issue quarterly or annual invoices in EUR with a DPA included as standard. If your procurement process requires a vendor security questionnaire, get in touch before signing.
What happens to my data if I cancel? ↓
Your data is never deleted on downgrade — the product goes read-only so you can still see your roadmap. On cancellation, you have 30 days to export everything via CSV before removal. We don't hold data hostage.
